Questions?

Q
What is the Harman Kardon Wireless HD Audio System?

The Harman Kardon Wireless HD Audio System is a premium suite of audio products designed to wirelessly fill your home with beautiful, high-definition sound. Start with any combination of Omni 10, Omni 20, or Adapt units and expand your system at any time to create seamless multi-room audio using your home wireless network and the Harman Kardon Controller App.

How does the system work?

Each speaker and adaptor wirelessly connects to your home Wi-Fi network. Using your phone or tablet and the Harman Kardon Controller App (available for iOS and Android), you can stream all your music through any speaker or any room in your home.

What is HD Audio?

The Harman Kardon Wireless HD Audio System streams music up to 24bit / 96kHz, which delivers unrivaled performance and better than CD quality.

Will the system work with my existing Hi-Fi equipment?

Yes. The entire system wirelessly connects to your home Wi-Fi network with just one power cable needed for each speaker. Other systems need to be wired to your router, or require an additional Bridge unit. Our system uses a special Firecast protocol, which allows your existing router to wirelessly send HD audio to every speaker. No additional equipment is needed.

Can I play through multiple speakers at the same time?

Yes. The Harman Kardon Wireless HD Audio System supports up to 12 speakers around your home which can be played individually, or linked together in the same room for multi-channel playback.

Can I use multiple speakers in the same room?

Yes, place any two Omni speakers in the same room and link them together for 2.0 stereo sound. Future updates will support more multi-channel configurations like 2.1, 5.0, and full 5.1 surround sound.

Should I choose an Omni 10 or Omni 20 for my room?

The Omni 10 is a smaller, elegant speaker designed to fill small to medium sized rooms. Start with one Omni 10 for rooms around 10 sqm, and add another for true 2.0 stereo sound in rooms up to 15 sqm.


The Omni 20 is a more powerful speaker designed to fill larger rooms. Use one Omni 20 in rooms up to 16 sqm, and add another for crystal clear 2.0 stereo sound in bigger rooms.

What music can I play?

With the HD Audio System, you can listen to all your music in all your rooms. Access any music stored on your phone or tablet, stream from a variety of services, like Spotify, integrated into the Harman Kardon Controller App (click here for a list of available services in your country), or use Bluetooth to re-stream audio from any device.

Do I need a bridge?

No. There’s no need to connect any device to your wireless router or a bridge. Apart from the power cable, the Harman Kardon Wireless HD Audio System is completely wireless.

What is the Link button?

The Link button on the top of every Omni speaker and Adapt lets you take your music from room to room with a single touch. Say you’re listening to an Omni 10 in one room and you walk into another room with an Omni 20. Just press the Link button and your music follows. It’s that simple.

And if you want to fill your entire home with sound, just press and hold the Link button to activate Party Mode to play the same song throughout every speaker and room in your home.

Can I stream using Bluetooth?

Yes. Connect your device to one speaker via Bluetooth and it can re-stream the same song through every other speaker in your system. Easily access all your music from any music source and get all the sound with none of the wires.

Do I need a home Wi-Fi network/router?

Yes. The entire system wirelessly connects to your home Wi-Fi network with just one power cable needed for each speaker. Other systems need to be wired to your router, or require an additional Bridge unit. Our system uses a special Firecast protocol, which allows your existing router to wirelessly send HD audio to every speaker. No additional equipment is needed.
